CLOVES LATIKA

Cloves latika or lavang latika is traditional Bengali sweet recipe prepared at the time of festivals in Bengal and some other places of India. This pocket sweet recipe is a bit time taking but easy to make is well. Mainly it is prepared from all purpose flour, khoya, sugar, dry coconut and some other spices. For making this delicious and mouthwatering labanga latika there are three major steps first is making stuffing, second its outer layer and third one is sugar syrup.

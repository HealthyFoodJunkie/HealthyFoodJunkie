CHANA JAISALMER KE

Chana Jaisalmer Ke is avery popular highly savoury cuisine of Rajasthan which has its origin from Jaisalmer. The cuisine is parepeare with black chick peas mixed with gram flour and curd (kadi). It is a type of kadi preparation with black peas/kala chana dipped in it. This incredible and popular dish is usually decorated with green dhania, red chilly and some desi ghee over top of it. This is consumed with rice or roti. The cuisine is also known as Jaisalmer Kala Chana Kadhi.

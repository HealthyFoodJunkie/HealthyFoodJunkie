ALOO POTALA RASA

ALOO POTALA RASAAlu Potala Rasa is a spicy gravy-based curry that originates from Odisha, India. It is made with potala (pointed gourd) and aloo (potatoes).A special dish prepared with potato and pointed gourd. It is one of the popular curries in the state of Orissa. Also known as Parwal in North India, this dish is offered to God at the Puri Jagannath temple. A bit of cashew paste or onion/ginger paste makes it more savoury.

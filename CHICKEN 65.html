CHICKEN 65

If there is one dish that is ubiquitous in India, mastered by roadside stalls and big restaurants alike, it is Chilli Chicken. A spicier version of this Chicken Chilli (with a decidedly South Indian twist of curry leaves and dried Kashmiri chilli), Chicken 65 is a favourite of the locals here. In Vijayawada and other parts of Andhra Pradesh, you’ll find Chicken 65 being sold at many local eateries and Chinese food stalls. It’s also a popular starter that is served at drinking joints, from mediocre bars to five-stars.

DALMOTH

A traditional namkeen, Dalmoth is a savoury dry snack that is a famous food of Agra. It is crispy, spicy and is made with lentils, nuts and spices. This snack is unique, and it is likely that you probably never had something like this before. This all time snack is a perfect accompaniment to a warm tea, a cool sherbet and even a chilled beer. Available all over Agra, our recommendation, however, is buying it at one of the original stores of Panchi Petha.

CHANA GHUGNI

Googhani/Gughni has it's origin from Uttar Pradesh(Eastern), Bihar and Calcutta. It is topped with finely chopped onions, dash of lemon juice. Many like to top it with puffed rice(murmura) or crispy flattened rice(chivda). One can top it with sev too and enjoy! Matra a popular version of Ghugni/Ghugri made with White Chickpeas is popular in Northern India especially Delhi region. It is served with special type of bread (Kulcha) which is different from Amritsari Kulcha. x

CHHURPI SOUP

This soup belongs to an ancient time. The soup is very well prepared in traditional style. It is widely available on any occasion. It is prepared by using either cow milk or yak milk. It is a dairy product. It tastes sour. It is used in snacks, and chhurpi powder is also used in its soups. It is also used in preparing pickles, and chhurpi pickles are famous worldwide.

BALUSHAHI

Crispy on the outside, moist and flaky on the inside, these deep-fried dough discs are known in north India for their unique texture. Balushahi is usually slow-cooked on a low flame, doused in a sugar syrup, and garnished with pistachios and almonds for an extra crunch. As this sweet can be stored for weeks, it’s a popular dessert at weddings and festivals, too

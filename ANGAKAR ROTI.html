ANGAKAR ROTI

Angakar Roti is a breakfast dish made in Bilarpur, using the staple flour of the region. The simplest version of this flatbread is made with just rice flour along with spices. Rice flour Roti tastes delicious with a spice side dish. When we had done the Indian Food Odyssey, where we cooked alphabetically from each Indian State, I had chosen to make Chusela, which is a popular rice pooris from Bilarpur, and had also mentioned the Rice Roti that's popularly known as the Angakar Roti, made with rice flour.

BANGULO CHICKEN

Most of the chicken aficionados in and around Visakhapatnam might have tasted this one by now. An unbelievable combination of multiple flavours coupled with a creative cooking technique, makes Bongulo Chicken is one of the best chicken recipes you can get. Famously made in the valleys of Araku, this spicy bamboo-stick-chicken dish promises a treat to the gourmet in you.

CHHOLE SAMOSE

CHHOLE SAMOSE no food pilgrimage in Allahabad can ever be complete without a trip to Sainik's in Ashok Nagar that sells the most flavorful and amazing Chhole Samose at Sainik Sweets. The traditional potato-filled samosa is crushed and the spicy chhole are added over it along with curds, some tamarind Chatni, onions, coriander and sev to make this legendary street food of Allahabad. It can be had a snack or even as a meal by itself. Don't try to pack it home though, the real taste of it lies in the hot steaming samosas and fresh chutneys.

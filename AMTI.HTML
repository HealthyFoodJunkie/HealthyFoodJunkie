AMTI

Amti is the staple dal curry (lentil soup) of Maharashtra and is made with tuvar or pigeon pea. What makes Pune’s amti different is a mix of spices called goda masala: a mix of red dried chillies, khus-khus (poppy seeds), dagadiphool or stone flower and finely shredded dried coconut.
